How often to you show up somewhere and need to have a question answered? Or perhaps you just want to discuss what is going on with other people that are also there? Sure you could talk to the person next to you but what are the odds that is the right person? What if you could find not only the right person but all of the right people to chat with?

Well, now you can!

With ChatSpot you can chat with everyone at your location or event. You simply connect to the ChatSpot 'room' for that place/event using any compatible mobile device or computer and your ready to start chatting!

FAQ

What device can I use with ChatSpot?
With the initial version of ChatSpot, all you need is a device that provides a modern web browser and you are set. That means that it works with iPhones, iPads, Android and Windows phones and tablets, your personal computer, and more.
How do I connect to ChatSpot 'room' for my place/event?
Good question! You access the list of nearby ChatSpot locations and events from a slide-out control panel. Select the place/event and you are ready to go.
